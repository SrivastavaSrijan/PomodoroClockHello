Effective methods

Pomodoro Technique.

Developed in the 1980’s by Francesco Cirillo, this time management technique operates on the belief that by dividing your work into short intervals, you can avoid feeling overwhelmed. It has also proven to be effective in preventing burn out.
  • Set a timer for 25 minutes, and start your task.
  • If a distraction pops into your head, write it down on a piece of paper and return to your task.
  • When the session is over, take a five minute break.
  • After four pomodoros, take a thirty-minute break.
  • Repeat!
